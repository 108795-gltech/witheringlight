Description

Withering Light is a 2D pixel-art adventure that places you in the mind of a student struggling through the effects of verbal bullying. You begin in a bright, peaceful garden, a reflection of childhood comfort, before following a drifting seed into a dark cave that symbolizes the fear and uncertainty of entering a new school.
Through light, darkness, and discovery, you experience how words can shape a person's world.
